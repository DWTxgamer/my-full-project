The German luxury-car legend is known for high-dollar, high-tech premium sedans, SUVs, coupes, wagons, and convertibles. At one end of spectrum is the CLA-class, which is a great subcompact sedan. Joining the CLA-class as one of our favorite compact sedans is the C-class. The E-class is terrific, with its effortless performance and peerless luxury for the price. The GLS is a great full-size SUV and a great value, but the flagship S-class sedan delivers the sybaritic luxury for which the brand is best known.

VISION EQXX: The new benchmark of efficiency.

The Mercedes-Benz Vision EQXX is a battery electric vehicle concept car that was unveiled at the 2022 Consumer Electronics Show. It is a proof of concept with a primary goal of below 10 kWh per 100 km (or greater than 10 km per kWh) in real-life conditions

Inspired by the future:The VISION AVTR.

The Mercedes-Benz Vision AVTR is a futuristic concept car inspired by the movie 'Avatar'. It features a 110 kilowatt-hour electric motor that can produce 469 horsepower and a range of roughly 435 miles.
